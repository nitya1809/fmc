Who We Are

Cinema is our
common language

The Film & Media Club at RGIPT is a space where students explore the art and craft of visual storytelling. Born out of a shared love for cinema, we bring together engineers, managers, and thinkers who believe every story deserves to be told well.

Located in Jais, Amethi — one of India's premier petroleum and energy institutes — FMC is proof that technical minds carry creative souls. Whether you're behind the camera, in the edit suite, or simply a passionate film watcher, there is a place for you here.

Our members have participated in national inter-college film festivals, produced short films and documentaries, and contributed to the visual identity of RGIPT's cultural fests and events.

What We Do

Every frame, a story

✦

Direction & Storytelling

From concept to screenplay, we nurture directorial vision and narrative craft through guided projects.

◎

Cinematography

Composition, lighting, and camera movement — we teach the visual language of film with hands-on shooting sessions.

▣

Editing & Post-Production

Colour grading, sound mixing, VFX basics — members shape raw footage into finished cinematic work.

◈

Documentary Filmmaking

We document campus life and untold stories — giving members real-world journalistic and cinematic experience.

◉

Photography

Still photography is at the heart of visual literacy. We cover events, conduct photowalks, and run critique sessions.

◇

Film Appreciation

Weekly screenings and discussions on cinema history, theory, and criticism — building a deeper understanding of the medium.
